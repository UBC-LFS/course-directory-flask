Structure of the Class:

  • This is a synchronous class. It will take place in person in the scheduled classroom. There will be no lectures via Zoom. In-person lectures will not be recorded, so it is in your best interest to come to class.
  • If you are unable to attend class, lecture material including slide annotations will be posted online after each lecture. 
  • Office hours are drop-in and are to be used to improve your understanding of course content.
  • This term we will be using Piazza for class discussion. The system is highly catered to getting you help quickly and efficiently from classmates, the TAs, and myself. Rather than emailing questions to the teaching staff, we encourage you to post your questions on Piazza.
