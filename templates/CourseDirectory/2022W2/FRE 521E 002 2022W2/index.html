As David Zilberman, one of my former professors at Berkeley said in a recent blog post, "... innovations are implemented through supply chains. Whoever designs a supply chain might establish institutions like markets, contracts, and other arrangements for trading. So, if you want to address problems like climate change, which will require drastic technological changes, you need to stimulate innovation that will be upscaled and propagated by supply chains." One of David's very recent papers -- the paper by Barrett et al. -- provides an overview of the development of agri-food value chains and serves as the background reading for this course.
