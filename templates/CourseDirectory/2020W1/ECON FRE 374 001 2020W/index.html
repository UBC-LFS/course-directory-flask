Structure of this Class

  • This is an online class. Pre-recorded videos and the lecture slides will be available on the Canvas page of this course. Please watch these videos every week.
  • In addition to those video lectures, we will have a synchronous (live) class on Mondays from 11:00 to 12:00 on Zoom.  In this live class, we will do exercises and discuss the topics covered in the prerecorded lectures.
  • The office hours are meant for informal discussions and questions. They are an important component of the course - so please attend them as often as possible.  There will be no extra office hours before the exam.
